About Me

I am a skilled data analyst with a proven track record of success in the pharmaceutical industry, boasting over three years of professional experience. My educational background includes a degree from Northeastern University, Boston, USA and I have also earned certification as a Scrum Master, demonstrating my commitment to agile methodologies and continuous improvement.

My career is driven by an insatiable appetite for challenges that enable me to refine my skills. I pride myself on my ability to fuse analytical prowess with creative thinking, a unique combination that has consistently propelled me to excel in my field. My unwavering passion for data fuels my dedication to staying at the forefront of industry trends and technology.

I am an avid learner, constantly seeking out new techniques and tools to elevate my skillset and tackle complex data-driven challenges head-on. Beyond my professional pursuits, I channel my creativity through the art of macrame cord, and immerse myself in the world of novels, where I find inspiration and relaxation.

Feel free to reach out to me, and let's discuss how my expertise and dedication to continuous improvement can be an asset to your team or project.
