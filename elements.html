About Me

I’m a data-focused professional with a strong academic foundation and cross-industry experience in analytics, technology, and leadership. I hold three master’s degrees: a Master of Professional Studies in Informatics from Northeastern University (2017–2019), a Master of Arts in Leadership (Business Stream) from Trinity Western University (2024–2025), and a Master of Science in Information Technology from Belhaven University (2024–2025).

Over the past several years, I’ve worked in highly regulated industries like pharmaceuticals, using tools such as Power BI, Tableau, Qlik Sense, and TrackWise to build dashboards, streamline reporting, and ensure data accuracy and compliance. My work has directly influenced business decisions, reduced manual effort, and improved operational transparency. I’m also certified in Scrum, bringing an agile mindset to team collaboration and continuous improvement.

At the core of my work is a passion for transforming data into stories that drive strategy. I specialize in identifying patterns, improving data quality, and aligning analytics solutions with business goals. Whether leading data initiatives or supporting cross-functional teams, I bring a balance of technical skill, leadership insight, and a deep appreciation for the impact of clean, actionable data.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I channel my creativity through the art of macrame cord, and immerse myself in the world of novels, where I find inspiration and relaxation.

Feel free to reach out to me, and let's discuss how my expertise and dedication to continuous improvement can be an asset to your team or project.
